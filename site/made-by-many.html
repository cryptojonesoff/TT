Why Table Talks started Made by Many.

Table Talks began as a supper club. It grew into a community, then a concept studio, running events built around connection.

But connection without care or generosity doesn't hold.

Events gave back to the cooks and the space — that worked.

It just wasn't enough. We want a stronger, more connected economy, not one good night.

Made by Many is the give-back arm of Table Talks. Brands and builders bring their ideas to life here, and what they build feeds directly back into the community it came from.

NOT A CAMPAIGN. A MISSION.

Every project we build sits inside something bigger.

A mission that grows, project by project, year by year, each one building on what came before. It's a portfolio of real things, growing together, funded and shaped by the people who show up for them.
