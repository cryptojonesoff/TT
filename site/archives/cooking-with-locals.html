Cooking with Locals

Siargao, Philippines
Culinary Experiences | Community Producers | Field Research

In collaboration with

Siargao's farmers, fishermen & home cooks

Not a single dinner — a standing collaboration with the people who grow, catch and cook Siargao's food every day.

Cooking with Locals sits inside Table Talks' Culinary Experiences mission category: chef collaborations, local producers and hands-on workshops, built alongside — not on top of — the island's own food culture.

The clearest expression of it is our Cookbook: content and recipes developed directly with community contributors — farmers, fishermen, home cooks — through field research, then produced and distributed with an established publisher. It's the same participation model behind everything Made by Many touches: discover a story worth telling, meet the people behind it, let the community back what matters, then document it properly and give it back.

This page is a live direction rather than a single dated event — as specific sessions, producer partners and recipes are confirmed, we'll update this page with the details.
