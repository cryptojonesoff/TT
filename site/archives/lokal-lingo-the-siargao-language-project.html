Lokal Lab

Language is one of the simplest ways to actually belong somewhere, not just visit it.

Lokal Lingo is a joint initiative between Made by Many and Lokal Lab, built to teach people the local language of Siargao through lessons and courses — lowering the barrier between visitors, newcomers and the community that's always been here.

Like every Made by Many project, it follows the same participation model: discover the opportunity, meet the people teaching and shaping it, let the community's contributions decide how it grows, then document and share it properly so it becomes something lasting rather than a one-off.

This page is an early direction rather than a finished programme — lesson formats, course dates and teaching partners are still being confirmed. We'll update this page with the real details as they're locked in.
