Table Talks is reactivating its Australian community — the same connective tissue that runs our Siargao missions, brought home to Melbourne.

Every Table Talks Australia event falls under one of four formats: Table Talks (CIF Missions) — "Gather. Share. Give." — community tables, culinary missions and community builds; Table Talks Salon — "Think. Discuss. Connect." — salon dinners, studio sessions and community roundtables; CIF Unbound Community — "Move. Explore. Experience." — adventure days, expeditions and field workshops; and CIF Waste & Sustainability — "Design. Make. Shape." — design workshops, maker sessions and collection launches.

Our flagship reactivation, working title "The first chapter — Designing better ways of living," is a curated cocktail salon at Moondrop — standing, three hours, 60–120 guests drawn from Melbourne's entrepreneurs, founders, architects, designers, hospitality leaders and creatives. Rather than formal presentations, the Community Impact Fund is introduced through atmosphere: at 7:30pm, the room live-streams straight from General Luna, Siargao, where Mission 001 is underway.

From there, the calendar continues into TT Salon: Kamayan x Palay on Smith St, Collingwood, a CIF Australia introduction and launch in Melbourne, a CIF Unbound launch under the Made by Many banner, and further Salon evenings built around the same idea — film, entrepreneurship, design, representation and hospitality as reasons to gather.

Every event, regardless of format, is built to the same standard: a minimum of 45 people for public gatherings or 12 for exclusive community events, one community partner, one venue partner or guest chef, one sponsor, and a room that includes influencers, media and industry voices — because every gathering here is also an asset that feeds back into the Community Impact Fund.
